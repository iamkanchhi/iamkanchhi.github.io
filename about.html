About Kanchhi

Trauma Survivor and surviving CPTSD

I am Kanchhi. I am a survivor.
I have survived
sexual abuse, emotional neglect, patriarchy and internalized misogyny...

Once a young little girl and now a grown woman.
It sounds normal, but I am not.
I am not a normal woman,
I am a woman who grew up with trauma without knowing it existed.
I am a woman who thought it was normal to feel the guilt for wanting to live as you please,
normal to feel the shame for being a woman so you must keep everything covered,
and to feel a burden to her parents who must be married off.
Yes I thought that feeling was normal.
I DID, in the past but NOT ANYMORE.

If you can resonate with that feeling, this page is for you.

My aim is to gather awareness for the hidden trauma a child can carry to adulthood..
I aim to also reach you, Kanchhi, another survivor.
I aim to tell you that you are not alone...
I aim to bring agency to you

Join me in crushing that masagynistic weight that was put on our free soul,
so we can all be free once again, as we were, as kanchhi was!

If you can resonate with that feeling, this page is for you. You're welcome here, exactly as you are.
