About Kanchhi

A short note on why this exists.

I am Kanchhi. I am a survivor.
I have survived
sexual abuse, emotional neglect, patriarchy and internalized misogyny...
Currently I am surviving C-PTSD.

Once a young little girl and now a grown woman.
It sounds normal, but I am not.
I am not a normal woman,
I am a women who has grown up with trauma without knowing it existed.
I am a woman who thought it was normal to feel the guilt when you want to live for yourself,
normal to feel the shame for being a woman so you must keep everything covered,
and to feel a burden to her parents who must be married off.
Yes I thought that feeling was normal.
I DID, in the past but NOT ANYMORE.

If you can resonate with that feeling, this page is for you.

My aim is to gather awareness for the hidden trauma a child can carry to adulthoo..
I aim to also reach you, Kanchhi, another young girl who was told she needs to be small because she is a woman.
one who was told to not dream because she need to fulfill her fathers, mothers and in the future a husbands dream.
Yes, we all believed it. Our moms believed it. Our grandmoms believed it.
NOT ANYMORE.

Join me in crushing that masagynistic weight that was put on our free soul,
so we can all be free once again like that little girl, Kanchhi!

If you can resonate with that feeling, this page is for you. You're welcome here, exactly as you are.
